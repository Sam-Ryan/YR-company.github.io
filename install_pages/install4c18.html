FlipBeats is a music player which lets you to listen to your iTunes music library & Cloud based Streaming Music Services in a very intuitive way. It takes the music listening to a whole new level by employing a “Studio Listening Experience” through a variety of PRO Equalizers, PRO Reverbs and Advanced Audio Configurations.

You can use predetermined Equalizer Presets based on what you are listening to, or fine-tune your sound stage by using the PRO EQ and PRO Reverbs engine to maximize the listening experience.
