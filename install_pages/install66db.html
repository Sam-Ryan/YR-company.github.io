MocaDeco is the easiest design
tool for mobile. Add eye-catching typography & design elements, apply dramatic filters & effects.

Key Features:

Project based editing
• Save your projects with fully editable and open them at any time. You can preserve all your design data and layers.

Gorgeous Graphics
• 700+ eye-catching artworks. Updating constantly
• Easily control size, color, opacity, position, rotation and add shadow.

Advanced Text Features
• Packed with 139 hand picked fonts. No additional purchase required.
• Easily add multiple text layers, controlling their size, positioning, opacity, rotation, & color.
• Control space between characters and lines.
• Make text pop with drop shadows

Starting with a Blank Canvas
• You can kicks off with a colored blank canvas instead of a photo.
• It is ideal for making graphics, logos, watermarks and more

Photo Magic
• Enhance your photos with 40+ sleek filter.

• Correct horizontal and vertical lines as well lens distortions.

Import and Export
• Import and Export your images in full resolution without sacrificing quality.
